She was a girl, and he was a boy. On a warm summer night, our two families were gathered for a father's day BBQ. She was there to inquire about this boy. He was there to inform her about ... teacher's college. She asked him about his hobbies, likes and dislikes while he dropped the question: "what is your five-year plan?"

Then a month went by...

"Do you like eating?"

He asked through text message. She laughed. She screenshotted it.

Against all odds, he did it, and she agreed to go out on a date.

Ever since their first date through Kensington Market where they demolished tacos, french-fries, and ice cream. They continue to demolish tacos, french-fries, and ice cream daily as they adventure to new districts, cities, and even countries.

Now, we are all here together at this moment where they will be taking their very next step of:
